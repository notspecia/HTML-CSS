Groudon, Kyogre, and Rayquaza

These three legendary Pokémon control the forces of nature: land, water, and air.

Groudon is the Pokémon that controls the land and the force of volcanoes. According to legend, it can expand the land by evaporating water. Its presence is associated with intense heatwaves and droughts.

Kyogre is the ruler of the oceans and controls torrential rains and storms. It is considered the creator of seas and the abundant rains that bring life to aquatic ecosystems. Its legend states that it can make the oceans grow immensely.

Rayquaza is the Pokémon that controls the skies and acts as a peacemaker between Groudon and Kyogre. Legend says it lives in the ozone layer and has come down to earth only to stop conflicts between the two legendaries. Rayquaza is seen as the protector of the sky and atmospheric tranquility.

Rayquaza

Rayquaza is a Dragon/Flying-type Legendary Pokémon.
