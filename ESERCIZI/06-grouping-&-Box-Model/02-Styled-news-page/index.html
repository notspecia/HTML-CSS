Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six: Siege (2014)

Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six: Siege (more commonly Rainbow Six: Siege and in some contexts shortened to R6S) is a tactical first-person shooter video game, focused on eliminating enemies, in this case attackers or defenders, primarily online.

It does not have a story mode but small missions to play in single player. Online features five game modes: New Recruit, Quick Match, Ranked, Unranked and Training Camp (playable in both online and offline modes). The maps are randomly chosen like the objectives (hostage, bomb and garrison).
